Research Focus | 研究方向

Quantum Computing

We develop superconducting qubits and scalable quantum architectures, with particular emphasis on hybrid systems combining circuit QED and emerging quantum materials.

研究重點包含超導量子位元、電路量子電動力學，以及結合新穎量子材料的混合量子系統。

Quantum Materials

Our quantum materials research explores two-dimensional and topological materials, focusing on their electronic, superconducting, and quantum coherent properties.

量子材料研究涵蓋二維材料與拓撲材料，著重其電子結構、超導性與量子相干特性。

Our Vision | 中心願景

The center aims to establish National Sun Yat-sen University as a leading hub for interdisciplinary quantum research in Asia, bridging fundamental science and future quantum technologies.

本中心目標是將中山大學打造為亞洲具影響力的跨領域量子研究樞紐， 串聯基礎科學與未來量子科技應用。
